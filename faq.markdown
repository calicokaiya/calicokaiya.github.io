**What is the meaning of "🐱" ?**

Though I will probably tell you that "🐱" stands for 🐱, there are many uses for the cat emoji, such as when making a joke, being ironic, or just because I felt that it would fit. All in all, I just really like the cat emoji.

**What does NSFL TRASH MXMMY mean / Why is that your name ?**

A lot of people ask me this because they do not know that NSFL is an acronym that stands for Not Safe For Life. It is typically used to tag scarring content. I came up with the name while having a mental breakdown. I am now in a better place.

I want to change it, but most people already know me as "MSFL TRASH MXMMY". I am looking for a good time to transition names.
